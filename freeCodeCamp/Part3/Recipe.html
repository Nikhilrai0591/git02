Matar Paneer Recipe

Matar Paneer is a classic North Indian dish loved for its rich taste, vibrant color, and comforting flavors. Made with soft cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and sweet green peas cooked in a spicy tomato-onion gravy, this recipe perfectly balances creaminess and warmth from aromatic Indian spices. Often enjoyed with roti, naan, or steamed rice, matar paneer is a popular choice for both everyday meals and special occasions.
